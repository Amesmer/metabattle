Overview

A power DPS Chronomancer build for fractals. This build deals very high burst and sustained damage against single targets. In addition it has very good CC, utility, and personal defense, and is capable of generating its own Might, Fury, Quickness, and Alacrity.

Be aware that this build requires high uptime of Slow on your target - you can take and use it during your if uptime is low.
