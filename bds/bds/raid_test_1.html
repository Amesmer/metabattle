Overview

Variant of Chronomancer - Utility Boon Support. This build fills the same role of Quickness and Alacrity but deals Torment and Confusion instead of power damage, which makes it perform better on bosses like Soulless Horror and Largos. In addition, this build can deal damage from range (useful for kiting agony on Cairn or doing ranged strat on Deimos) and its conditions increase the damage of teammates via traits like and .
