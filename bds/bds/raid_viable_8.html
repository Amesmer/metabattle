Overview

A power DPS build for Herald. One of the very first raid DPS builds in the Heart of Thorns era, this build has now returned to viability, providing solid sustained damage, good CC, Vulnerability and .
While not the best choice on any one encounter, Power Herald has a simple, easy to understand rotation making it an ideal build for newer players to learn encounters on.

Notes

  • Be careful not to interrupt your auto-attack chain as much of the damage from it is loaded onto the final strike.
  • You should only swap to staff if breakbar contribution is needed as swapping is a major damage loss.
  • Facet of Nature is generally only useful to maintain on and even then it is effectively a necromancer's Vampiric Aura that costs you approximately 1000 personal DPS.
