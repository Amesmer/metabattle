Slot Changes

Utility

For the utility slot:

  • - damage mitigation and useful boonstacking, especially against large aoe or in group settings
  • - if you want a panic button that helps you live through any amount of direct damage.

For the elite slot:

  • - more selfish option. Can immobilize opponents for potentially long periods of time, which can be useful in landing burst or rarely to keep an opponent in place while you decap a node.

Pet options:

  • Juvenile Rock Gazelle slightly tankier burst pet that provides cc.

If you do not own any of the expansions take the following pets (these pets are also viable if you do own them):

  • Juvenile Raven bird with high consistent damage and blind
  • Juvenile Owl as a second bird. Has consistent damage and chill
  • Juvenile Tiger burst pet. Has a high damage low cd burst skill that also provides fury uptime.
