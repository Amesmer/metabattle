Overview

A condition DPS build for Mirage. This build provides very high single target damage with the ambush skill . It excels against bosses that move or attack a lot due to a large portion of its damage coming from Confusion and Torment. It is also highly survivable and can easily avoid enemy attacks because it is in for most of its rotation.
This guide is focused on fractals. For the raid version see Mirage - Condi DPS Chaos.
