Overview

Burn DH is a build that provides high damage output, strong cleanses, and reasonable crowd control in zerg fights. This build is an improvement over the old-school base-burn guard in that it offers: more powerful CCs through the use of longbow, an abundance of soft CCs such as cripple, slow and chill, greater mobility and self-sustain through the use of dragonhunter, ability to boon rip, and generally higher damage compared to core mainly due to the numerous ways of covering your burn conditions and by having access to the longbow which generates more burn and ticks than the staff.


In addition to modifying traits, the sigils and stat prefixes have been overhauled. Namely, a full set of trailblazer is selected over a mixture of dire/trailblazers. While full TB has slightly less condition damage (about 3% less burn damage), it caps the burn duration and allows the player to run more useful sigils like bursting which actually gives you a slighter higher burn tick compared to the previous arrangement, and sigil of absorption to steal boons. Furthermore, the additional expertise augments your chill, cripple and slow durations and forces the enemy to expend more cleanses rather than letting it expire naturally. Lastly, sigil of corruption has been added to the secondary weapon set over sigil of fire due to the enormous damage boost provided by 250 condition damage.
