Overview

A Tempest build focused on boon support in dungeons and fractals. This build provides 25 Might and Fury to its party while still dealing respectable DPS for a support class.
If needed, it can change its skills and traits to provide Vigor and Protection at a significant DPS tradeoff (see "Auramancer Variant" below).
The boons provided by this build are generally redundant with Chronomancer - Utility Boon Support (Fractal) or Druid - Boon Support Healer (Fractal), making it a poor choice in organized team comps. However, it can be a good option in disorganized dungeons or fractals when a chronomancer or druid isn't available.
