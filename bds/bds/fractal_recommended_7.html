Equipment

The recommended gear setup will take you to 85% boon duration, which is enough to maintain permanent alacrity with just casting on cooldown with no other alacrity sources. Note that it assumes you will be using the buffs from your Fractal Potions with 150 Agony Resistance, so if you don't regularly use fractal potions you will need to gain extra concentration from your gear.
