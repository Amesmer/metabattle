Overview

Variant of Chronomancer - Utility Boon Support, specialized for tanking in high-damage raid encounters. This build provides the same amount of boon coverage while also having very high personal survivability and good group healing. In fights that place a large amount of damage pressure on the tank, this build can self-sustain indefinitely, placing less pressure on the healer(s) and ensuring a safer (if slightly slower) clear.

Equipment

There are many variations of gear for Chronomancer and some of them can be difficult to acquire, however they all share a common goal in their stat choices which is to reach enough boon duration to maintain sufficient boons for their group. The required boon duration will depend on the skill of you and your group, as well as whether you're running with builds such as Firebrand - Quickness Support Power DPS and Renegade - Boon Support Power DPS.
The suggested gear here provides you with an extreme amount of boon duration to ensure boons stay up even with things being less than ideal (including which also gives you a lot of boon duration). If you want you can run more Nomad's gear and drop to as low as 25% boon duration while still maintaining boons, however that is not recommended unless you are very confident that mistakes won't occur.
Remember to get a sword, focus, and shield offhand.
