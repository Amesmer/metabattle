Overview

Dragonhunter is a heavy burst damage power DPS that excels in short fights (such as fractals) and raid encounters with short damage windows. It has strong CC and good mobility, and provides Vulnerability. In addition, it brings very high defensive support for a DPS class, with a party wide block in , a strong burst heal in , and the ability to bring Guardian utilities such as and .
Dragonhunter is somewhat reliant on Support Chronomancers or other Guardians to help it maintain Retaliation.

Weapon Variants

  • Scepter provides ranged damage, which can be useful and is recommended on bosses where sword might be too risky, e.g. Deimos with the Rapid Decay mechanic, or where mechanics might force you out of melee range, e.g. Xera (clearing shards), Dhuum (Death Marks), and Cardinal Adina (pillars).
