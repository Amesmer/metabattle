Overview

A condition DPS and utility build for Scourge. While not the highest DPS build, this build provides decent sustained DPS and ridiculously strong AoE cleave with . It also provides strong incidental support for its party by generating Might, cleansing conditions, and overhealing allies with barriers.
While Scourge is not normally considered a strong DPS class, its defensive support makes it great for carrying weaker groups. It can fill an off-support role in the line of Druid - Boon Support Condi DPS as it can both heal and do good damage. Note that barrier is unaffected by the healing reduction of Agony, which means in some situations a Scourge will heal for more than a dedicated healer.
This guide is focused on fractals. For the raid version see Scourge - Utility Condi DPS.
