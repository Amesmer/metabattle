Overview

A condition DPS and utility build for Scourge. While not the highest DPS build, this build provides decent sustained DPS and ridiculously strong AoE cleave with . It also provides strong incidental support for its subgroup by generating Might, cleansing conditions, and overhealing allies with barriers.
Scourge is mainly a utility class and excels in encounters where it can play to its strengths, namely and defensive support. It also deals relatively high ranged damage and isn't reliant on Might or healing, which makes it good for roles such as flak kiting at Sabetha.
This guide is focused on raids. For the fractal version see Scourge - Utility Condi DPS (Fractal).
