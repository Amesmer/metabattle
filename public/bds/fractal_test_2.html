Overview

A healing variant of Chronomancer - Utility Boon Support (Fractal). This build compresses the roles of Support Chronomancer and Healing Druid by providing healing, Might, Fury, Quickness, and Alacrity to a fractal party.
While this build provides strong Regeneration, it has unreliable burst healing and shouldn't be thought of as a primary healer. Its main use is in highly experienced fractal groups that don't need much healing and wish to replace the healer with a 3rd DPS to push out more damage.
This build is highly situational and a new Chronomancer player shouldn't make it as their first gear set. Chronomancer - Utility Boon Support (Fractal) is better for new players and more generally useful.

Equipment

There are many variations of gear for Chronomancer and some of them can be difficult to acquire, however they all share a common goal in their stat choices which is to reach as close as possible to 100% boon duration. This gear assumes you will be gaining extra boon duration from with 10 boons and having 150 agony resistance and the Fractal Mobility Potion - if you don't have that combination you will be below 100%.
