Overview

A Soulbeast WvW roamer focusing on boon stacking, mobility and sustain. While it's primarily made for 1v1s, the build has several tools for buffing allies as well when roaming in a group.

Usage

Elite specialization basics

  • Soulbeast allows Rangers to meld with their pet, becoming one. This is done through which has a 10s CD.
  • Soulbeasts can't swap pets in combat. Use Owl if you want more defense, Smokescale if you want more damage.
  • Pet related traits and skills such as and will continue to function even while merged, but this time you'll be the one who benefits from them, not the pet (although sometimes the effects are lessened).


General

  • and are your two main mobility skills. Make sure you do not have anyone targeted while using them. Make sure you do not have an enemy targeted when using these skills for general mobility or disengaging.
  • passively maintains a high uptime of healing reduction in duels via Poison, which is very important when fighting other bunker specs.
  • Dodging applies Protection, which also heals thanks to the trait . also reduces the condition damage you take by 33% while affected by this boon.
  • Survival skills each cure 2 conditions and grant Fury. Troll Unguent also provides 2 seconds of Resistance thanks to the rune.
  • Each pet provides a different kind of immunity. Juvenile Smokescale has Smoke Assault against direct damage, while Juvenile Owl offers Resistance against conditions via . The latter also heals for a huge amount and it's AoE, so try to share it with your team whenever possible! Now that Soulbeast can only use one pet your main choice will be Juvenile Owl for the extra heal and mobility, don't forget to unmerge when you don't need beast skills as you have greater DPS while you let your pet attack them.
