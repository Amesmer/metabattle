Usage

Elite specialization basics

  • Dodge rolling is replaced by Mirage Cloak, which is more or less the same thing but allows you to cast skills and grants access to ambush attacks over its duration: for Axe and for Sword. Ambush skills replace the autoattack of the weapons for 1.5 seconds.
  • You can dodge while casting skills or CC'd, but only have one dodge bar.

Mobility

  • Use when moving between capture points, assuming you get your endurance back before making it to the next fight. Throwing in a for the Vigor from could help you recover endurance a bit quicker, further boosting your mobility by a small amount.


Damage

  • Sustained damage is fairly low in this build, while burst damage is extremely high.
  • Your goal is to roam the map, find a target that's out of CDs, and take them down in a matter of seconds before moving on.
  • Axe is the primary offensive set while Sword is a bit more defensive.
