Overview

A Condi Roaming with +Healing Power and does not use the Trickery specialty that everyone seems to think is necessary for any build. Main focus is on chaining stealth attacks to increase damage from poison. The combination of the 3 results in very fast TTKs against all but the most tanky of builds, while being deceptively tanky oneself.
