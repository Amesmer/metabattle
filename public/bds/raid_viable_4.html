Overview

A pistol/pistol spamming Deadeye build.
Despite being one of the oldest memes in Guild Wars 2, this build deals decent sustained ranged damage, isn't reliant on Alacrity or Might uptime, and is far more mobile than Deadeye - Power DPS Rifle. This makes it viable for kiting mechanics (such as flak on Sabetha) or doing ranged strat on Deimos.
If you are actually able to melee the boss Deadeye - Power DPS D/D will usually perform better.
