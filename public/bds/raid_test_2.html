Usage

The main facet of this build is to use with three clones up as often as possible, to apply as much confusion as possible, in combination with scepter skills and torch and axe skills. Three clones will reduce its cooldown to 10 seconds, which will be further reduced by alacrity, allowing you to shatter every 6-7 seconds or so.
