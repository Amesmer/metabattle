Every Grandmaster tier trait is viable depending on the situation:

  • adds to your AoE, you're free to take it if the enemy team doesn't have significant condition pressure.
  • works best against enemy comps with heavy condition pressure but no boon removal.
  • is somewhere inbetween the other two traits, mixing both defense and offense. Best option when facing condition builds with boon removal such as Necromancers other Mallyx players.
